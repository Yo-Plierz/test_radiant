The Story in the Book

Chapter 1

Molly had been staring out of her window for about an hour now.On her desk,lying between the copies of Nature,New Scientist,and all the other scientific journals her work had appeared in,was well thumbed copy of On the Road. it had bee molly's favorite favorite book since college,and the longer she spent in these four walls the more she felt she needed to be free.

She had spent the last ten years in the room,sitting under a poster with an oscar Wlde quote proclaiming that "Work is the refuge of people who have nothing better to do".Altough many considered her pionerring work,unravelling the secrets of the llama DNA,to be an outstanding achievement, Molly did think she had something better to do.
